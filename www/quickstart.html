Assumptions

We use maven and JBoss, but it should also work with ant and any other JMS container.

Server Setup

  1. download JBoss 6.0.0.M5
  2. unzip it to a directory you like (we call this ${jboss})
  3. start ${jboss}/bin/run.sh
  4. wait for ... JBossAS [6.0.0.20100911-M5 "Neo"] Started in 32s:736ms
  5. open admin console
  6. log in (admin/admin is the default)
  7. click in the navigation tree on JBossAS Servers/JBoss AS 6 (default)/Applications/Web Application (WAR)s
  8. click on the button on the right Add a new resource
  9. click on the Browse... button, select the war you just created, and click Open
  10. open your war
